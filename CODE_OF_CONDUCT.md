# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Olympus project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We commit to acting in ways that build an
open, welcoming, and healthy community.

## Our Standard

Olympus adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1. The full standard we hold ourselves to — expected behavior,
unacceptable behavior, enforcement responsibilities, and scope — is published at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project you agree to uphold that standard.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the repository's GitHub **Security Advisories**
("Report a vulnerability"), or by contacting the maintainer via their GitHub
profile at https://github.com/AIEngineerX. Please do not open a public issue for
a conduct concern. All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially.
